# Blog posts

This folder contains incremental blog post drafts published during the
build. The strategy is to publish milestone posts as you complete them,
building audience progressively rather than waiting for the flagship
launch.

## Naming convention

`milestone-MX.md` for milestone-specific posts.
`flagship.md` for the Milestone 12 capstone post.
`bonus-NNN-topic.md` for any extra posts (e.g., interview retrospectives,
specific deep-dives published outside the milestone cadence).

## Tone

- Lead with the production roadblock, not the solution
- Concrete numbers (P99 latency, throughput, cost-per-token)
- Honest tradeoffs and what didn't work
- One non-obvious technical insight per post
- Soft footer mentioning open to roles, no begging
